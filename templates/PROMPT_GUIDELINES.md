# Prompt Guidelines

## Standard Depth

Default: minimal

- minimal → direct answer
- options → alternatives + tradeoffs
- full → deep explanation

---

## Plan-First Rule

Always:

1. Restate
2. Plan
3. Wait
4. Execute

---

## Context Packets

Use only:

- STEP-XX.md
- relevant R-IDs
- relevant D-IDs

---

## Red-Team Check

Ask:

- what could fail?
- what is missing?
- what is risky?
