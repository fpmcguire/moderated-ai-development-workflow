# MOD-W with Gemini

> See the full integration guide: [`articles/modw-with-gemini.md`](../../articles/modw-with-gemini.md)

Gemini can be used to support the **Designer** role in MOD-W, producing `DESIGN-SPEC.md` from `PRODUCT.md`, `ROADMAP.md`, and visual assets. Gemini **Gems** and **NotebookLM** provide role separation and persistent project context.

See [`README.md`](../../README.md) for the Gemini section, and [`articles/`](../../articles/) for the detailed playbook.
